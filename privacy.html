Privacy Policy — Okmus

Last updated: July 3, 2025

Okmus ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile game developed by Talha Cosar. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personal information. However, our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob uses advertising identifiers (such as IDFA on iOS and GAID on Android) to serve relevant advertisements. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Data Storage

Okmus stores game progress (level completion and unlocked levels) locally on your device using shared preferences. No data is transmitted to external servers by us.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
